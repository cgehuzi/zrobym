INTERIOR

If you have visited our web-site it means that you have faced a problem of contractor choice for an interior design of a flat, office or commercial space. Projecting is the toughest task demanding professional knowledge and skills. That is why it is very significant to choose a right design studio. We have been dealing with the interior designs development and implementation of apartments, houses and commercial space. They are of diverse complexity levels.

For some people the term “Interior design” is significant and just connected with a set of beautiful pictures. For us it is creating of aesthetic and functional space properties, making up of a successive instruction to repair implementation. In our design project it is precisely pointed what, where and at what price to buy. An interior design project development is a complicated process, where a big group of contractors is involved. The function of a design studio is to coordinate all this process participants’ actions for a project completion.
